About Free AI Text Tools

Building free, powerful text and SEO tools for everyone.

Who We Are

Free AI Text Tools is a free online platform providing 37 AI-powered text and SEO utility tools. Our suite includes word counter, JSON formatter, meta tag generator, keyword density checker, Markdown editor, robots.txt generator, and many more tools designed to make your work easier and more efficient.

Founded in 2026, we serve writers, developers, SEO professionals, students, marketers, and anyone who works with text on a daily basis.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: make professional-grade text tools accessible to everyone, completely free. We believe that essential productivity and SEO tools should not be locked behind paywalls or require lengthy sign-up processes. Every tool on our platform is available instantly, works entirely in your browser, and costs nothing to use.

What We Offer

Our platform covers a wide range of text and SEO utilities organized into five main categories:

  • Text Tools: Word counter, character counter, sentence counter, paragraph counter, duplicate line remover, case converter, text reverser, line sorter, and remove extra spaces.
  • Counters: Word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, and reading time estimates.
  • Encoders & Decoders: URL encoder/decoder, HTML encoder/decoder, and Base64 encoder/decoder.
  • Developer Tools: JSON formatter, JSON validator, XML formatter, XML validator, Markdown editor with live preview, and robots.txt generator.
  • SEO Tools: Meta tag generator, Open Graph generator, sitemap generator, canonical tag generator, slug generator, meta description generator, keyword density checker, and keyword extractor.

Why Choose Us

  • Privacy-First: All text processing happens directly in your browser. Your data never leaves your device — guaranteed.
  • No Sign-Up: No accounts, no registrations, no email requirements. Just open a tool and start working.
  • Lightning Fast: Since everything runs locally in your browser, results are instant. No server round-trips, no waiting.
  • Mobile-Friendly: Every tool is fully responsive and works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
  • Dark Mode: Easy on the eyes with a built-in dark mode toggle for comfortable use in any lighting.
  • Completely Free: No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no limits. All 37 tools are 100% free forever.
