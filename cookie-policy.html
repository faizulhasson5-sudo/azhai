What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences.

Cookies We Use

Necessary Cookies

  • theme_preference: Remembers your light/dark mode selection
  • cookie_consent: Remembers your cookie acceptance or rejection

Analytics Cookies

  • _ga, _ga_* (Google Analytics): Anonymous usage statistics

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Our cookie banner (shown on first visit)
  • Your browser settings
  • Browser developer tools

Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to collect anonymous usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
